About Dr. Jones

Dr. Jeffrey R. Jones is an innovative neuroscientist joining the University of Florida with extensive experience from the Salk Institute for Biological Studies, where he worked in the laboratory of renowned scientist Fred H. Gage.

His groundbreaking research focuses on understanding the cellular mechanisms of aging and their contributions to cognitive decline, with particular expertise in DNA damage and cellular metabolism. Dr. Jones brings a multidisciplinary molecular biology skill set that includes cutting-edge techniques in genetic engineering, stem cell biology, and advanced genomics.

As former Interim Director of Salk's Stem Cell Core (2021-2022), Dr. Jones has demonstrated exceptional leadership in advancing stem cell research and fostering collaborative scientific environments.
