Neuronal Pathway Regulation

This study explores how XYZ pathway regulates neuronal activity and contributes to brain plasticity.

We found that modulation of this pathway significantly altered synaptic activity. These findings provide insight into potential therapeutic strategies for neurodegenerative conditions.

Astrocytic Conversion Models

We developed a novel model for direct astrocytic conversion, providing new insights into brain repair.

Our experiments show enhanced GFAP expression in fibroblast-to-astrocyte reprogramming. This lays groundwork for regenerative medicine applications in neurodegenerative diseases.
